**Test Cases:**

**TC_001: U1.1F1 - View Registration Page of Patient**

*   **Description:** Verify that the system displays the registration page for patients.
*   **Steps:**
    1. Launch the application and navigate to the login page.
    2. Click on the "Register" button.
    3. Verify that the registration page is displayed with the required fields (e.g., name, email, password).
*   **Expected Result:** The system displays the registration page with the required fields.

**TC_002: U2.2F2 - Book Appointment Online with Diagnostic Center**

*   **Description:** Verify that the system allows patients to book an appointment with a diagnostic center.
*   **Steps:**
    1. Launch the application and navigate to the login page.
    2. Login as a patient.
    3. Click on the "Book Appointment" button.
    4. Select a diagnostic center and a doctor.
    5. Verify that the system displays the available time slots.
    6. Select a diagnostic center and a doctor, and a time slot.
    7. Verify that the system handles the booking successfully.
*   **Expected Result:** The system allows patients to book an appointment with a diagnostic center and displays the available time slots.
